Design Style Description

The overall design style is whimsical, playful, and family-oriented, aimed at a parenting audience. Key elements include:

    Color Palette: A soft, inviting palette of teal, pastel yellow, muted coral/orange, pale pink, and cream.

    Typography: Bold, rounded, and slightly chunky sans-serif fonts used for titles, paired with clean, readable sans-serif text for descriptions.

    Shapes & Layout: Everything features highly rounded corners—from the app logo to the large card containers and the inner illustration squares.

    Illustrations: The graphics are flat, vector-style, and feature hand-drawn elements like squiggles, stars, clouds, and simple icons (e.g., a book, sound waves, a pencil, a car).

    Overall Vibe: The layouts are clean and organized but feel soft, approachable, and "fun," avoiding rigid or corporate design conventions.
